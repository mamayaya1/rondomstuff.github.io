Mojang's celebration for the 10 year anniversary of MineCraft, Classic Minecraft, is a recreation of one of the beta builds of the game Minecraft. And now you can play it unblocked! Use your mouse (or touch pad) to look around and use WASD to move. Use the left click to place a block and right click to switch to breaking blocks. Finally you can click B to get more blocks. 

This version actually has more blocks too! Just be warned, if you try to change up the settings and your mouse gets stuck, press ¨esc¨ to get it back. It is annoying but it works.
